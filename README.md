# Lead.AI Business Audit

AI-powered business automation audit and roadmap generator for small businesses.

## Status

Planned

This repository currently contains the product foundation and documentation structure. It does not yet include a runnable application.

## Product Vision

Lead.AI Business Audit will help business owners identify automation opportunities, score operational gaps, and generate a practical roadmap for AI-enabled workflows.

## Planned Capabilities

- Guided business intake form.
- Automation opportunity scoring.
- AI-generated workflow recommendations.
- Priority roadmap by effort, impact, and risk.
- Exportable audit summary for business owners.

## Intended Users

- Small business owners.
- Consultants and automation agencies.
- Operators evaluating where AI can save time or improve customer response.

## Technology Direction

- Web application for intake and report generation.
- AI workflow for structured recommendation output.
- Report storage and export support.
- Clear privacy boundaries for submitted business data.
